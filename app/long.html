Violent Tornadoes

What are 'Violent Tornadoes'

Tornadoes of F4/f5/EF-4/EF-5 status are considered violent tornadoes. These tornadoes produce winds from 166mph to over 200mph. These are wind estimates based on damage from 28 indicators outlined by the National Weather Service.
